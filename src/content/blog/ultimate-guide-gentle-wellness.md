Wellness culture loves to tell you to wake up at 5 AM, cold plunge, and journal for an hour. But when you're managing a chronic condition — endometriosis, fibromyalgia, autoimmune issues — that advice isn't just unhelpful, it's harmful.

This guide is about building a wellness practice that actually works with your body, not against it. No guilt. No performance. Just small things that add up on the days you have capacity.
